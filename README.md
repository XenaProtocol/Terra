# 🌱 Terra 🌱

**Because gardening shouldn't be a full-time job.**

Welcome to **Terra**! A streamlined, automated gardening manager for Final Fantasy XIV designed to take the tedious clicking out of tending your crops. Whether you are crossbreeding Thavnairian Onions or just keeping your FC's garden alive, Terra handles the heavy lifting.

## ✨ Features

* 🎛️ **Farming Controller:** A clean, easy-to-use control panel that lets you Fertilize, Water, and Harvest entire beds with a single click.
* 🐟 **Smart Fertilizer Selection:** Easily select which fertilizer to use (like Fishmeal) directly from the dropdown menu before applying it to your beds.
* 📊 **Live Progress Tracking:** Keep an eye on your gardening progress with a built-in status indicator and percentage bar.
* ⏱️ **Advanced Timing Settings:** Customize the delays and timing of your actions to suit your playstyle and ensure the game registers every action safely.
* 📍 **Character & Location Info:** Conveniently displays your current class, level, and location (like The Lavender Beds) right in the UI.

## 🎮 How to Use

1. Open the game and type `/frm` in your chat to open the Terra control panel.
2. Stand near the garden patch you want to tend.
3. Select your desired fertilizer from the dropdown list.
4. Click **Fertilize Bed**, **Water Bed**, or **Harvest Bed** and let Terra do the work!

## 🛠️ Installation

*Since this plugin is currently in development, you can easily install Terra by adding my custom repository to your Dalamud settings!*

1. Open FFXIV and type `/xlsettings` in the chat.
2. Go to the **Experimental** tab.
3. Scroll down to **Custom Plugin Repositories**.
4. Paste this link into an empty box: `YOUR_CUSTOM_REPO_LINK_HERE`
5. Click the **+** button and make sure the "Enable" box is checked.
6. Click **Save and Close** at the bottom.
7. Open `/xlplugins`, search for **Terra**, and click Install!
